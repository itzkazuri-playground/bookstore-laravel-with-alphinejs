## **C.4 Questions**

### **Project Name** : Please make a simple web example with specification below

### **Client story** :

John Doe is a client who have a business of bookstore which his main business is to rent or sell books. 

He ask our help to recap all of his books collection so he can sort it from the most popular ones.  So, with this sorted data, he can give his customer best experience in giving suggestion what is the best books in store he have based on most popular book or most popular author.

Every time customer come, he will ask them to ***give rating on the book*** they buy or borrow.

### **Expected result website ( 3 Pages )**

- List of books with filter
- Top 10 most famous author
- Input rating

**Notes** : 

Voter is count of how many person give rating to books

The scale of rating is from 1 to 10

---

### **C.4.1 List Data of Books**

- Filter by:
    - Category (multiple selection with AND/OR logic)
    - Author
    - Publication year range
    - Availability status (available, rented, reserved)
    - Store location
    - Rating range (e.g., 7-10)
- Search by: Book title, Author name, ISBN, Publisher
- Sorting options:
    - Weighted average rating (default)
    - Total votes
    - Recent popularity (last 30 days)
    - Alphabetical
- **Pagination**: Must efficiently handle 200k records
- Display:
    - Book info (title, author, categories, ISBN)
    - Current average rating + total voters
    - Trending indicator (↑ if rating improved in last 7 days)
    - Availability status

---

### **C.4.2 List of top 20 most famous author,** with multiple ranking tabs:

- **By Popularity**: Voters count (rating > 5 only)
- **By Average Rating**: Overall quality
- **Trending**: Authors gaining momentum (comparing last 30 days vs previous 30 days)

**Additional Requirements:**

- Show author statistics:
    - Total number of ratings received
    - Best-rated book
    - Worst-rated book

**Logic Challenge:**

- Calculate "trending score" = ( difference of average between recent month versus last month ) x Weight of voter count

---

### **C.4.3 Input rating**

- Every input here is a drop down input
- ***Please make sure, the book name input here is created by those author***
- The rating is number increment by 1, from the lowest is 1 to 10
- After submit success, please go back to first page ( list of book )

**Important Rules:**

- Handle concurrent submissions
- Handle duplicate rating attempts gracefully
- Users must wait 24 hours between ratings (any book)
- Handle invalid book-author combinations
- Show meaningful error messages
